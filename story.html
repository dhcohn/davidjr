David Junior appeared to me, David Senior, in a series of three dreams. In the first dream, he revealed to me his genderless form. In the second dream, he explained that he emerged from the deep unconscious of western society. In the third, he outlined the three tenets of his prophecy. Upon awakening from the third dream, I found that I had excreted him into the world. We live together now and I deliver his message.

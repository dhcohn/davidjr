David Junior appeared to me, David Senior, in a series of three dreams. In the first dream, he revealed to me his genderless form. In the second dream, he explained his origins. In the third, he outlined the three tenets of his prophecy. Upon awakening from the third dream, I found him lying next to me. We live together now and I deliver his messages to the world.

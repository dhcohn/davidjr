There is a way to mathemetically describe the idea that the only constant is change, that all differences are also different from themselves. This constant is known as ‘e’. The ‘∂’ symbol means a quantitative difference. The symbol ‘d’ here means deferral, or qualitative difference, which is like the bit of time between ‘A’ and ‘B’. The symbol ‘r’ here means relationality, which is like the amount that ‘A’ is still connected to itself (sometimes called trace in philosophy). You can think of ‘r’ as the amount that you are still you when going from one surface to another. You can think of ‘d’ as the amount you change when you pursue another surface out of some desire. The equation is r=ke^d where k is any value that stays the same.

Let's say ‘A’ and ‘B’ and ‘C’ are pieces of meaning. They each have a function in language, which works according to different rules in different situations. Meaning is a little slippery since ‘A’ can mean ‘B’ and ‘B’ can mean ‘C’, just like the fact that when you open up a dictionary and see a word, it doesn't just say the same word in the definition. Instead, it gives a string of other words, which are also somewhere else in the dictionary. And round and round like that so that no piece of meaning is itself. So what holds everything in place? And how do we communicate? All good questions!

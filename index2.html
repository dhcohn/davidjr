About David Junior:

David Junior is an immaterial being who navigates a series of networked surfaces in an imaginary universe. This universe is flat, inside-out, filled with differences of all kinds, but is everywhere the same. David Junior has been sent to show its other inhabitants — you and me —the structure of this universe.

There are some people who still believe that the earth is flat and their belief is incidentally a correct one. But this is because the whole universe is flat. It is only a surface, or more accurately an infinite surface twisted in on itself in a mobius fashion, much like David Junior's personality. The twist is not in one place though. Instead, it is at every location such that the whole thing is inside-out and such that each point is already somewhere else. What the jibber-jabber does this mean for you and me? Follow David Junior and he'll explain.
